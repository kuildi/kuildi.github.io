About us

Located in Middlebury, Connecticut, L is an inviting 90 seat restaurant owned and operated by accomplished Chef Linda Sirica. "Like you, I enjoy food that satisfies the soul as well as the palate," says Linda. "Food excites the imagination along with the taste buds. So, I invite you to experience food that holds the promise of a good story!"

We are offer exceptional gourmet American Cuisine with an Italian flair - served in a warm atmosphere where the emphasis is on comfort, taste and above all, relaxation and enjoyment. Grilled NY Strip with Baked Mac n Cheese and Sweet Onion Jam laced Demi-Glace; Angelhair pasta with Shrimp, diced tomatoes, spinach, roasted red peppers & lemon vodka cream sauce; and Grilled Salmon with farro & cranberry salad with mustard sauce are just a few of the special dishes you'll find on L's enticing menu. Cap off your main course with a Cappuccino and one of our specialty desserts such as Banana Napoleon or Creme Brulee. Enjoy a glass of fine wine, or indulge in a specialty martini or after-dinner cordial.

At Delicious, we turn the ordinary into the extraordinary every day. So, stop in for a romantic dinner, relaxing lunch, or after work gathering at our cozy, sit-down bar...make everyday life a little more special at Delicious Restaurant!
